Making French Toast

  1. Get a somewhat large bowl for mixing
  2. Add a couple cups of milk (or prefered subsitute) and crack open two eggs into the bowl
  3. Begin mixing with a whisk
  4. Place a pan on the stove, spray with cooking spray and begin heating at medium temp
  5. Whip out your bread, preferably Texas Toast, and dip both sides of the toast into the mix
  6. Begin cooking both side of the toast to your prefered level of toasty-ness
  7. Once to your liking, place toast on plate and feel free to add either sugar, butter, or syrup
  8. Enjoy!

Minesweeper Map Generator

  1. Ask for user input to choose board size
  2. Ask for another input for number of bombs
  3. For every bomb, randomly place one in a grid until all have been placed
  4. For every non-bomb tile, detect how many bomb tiles are touching it
  5. Assign non-bomb tiles a number based on how many bombs are nearby
  6. Print out the grid visually
